Create Ayushman Bharat Health ID

Create ABHA ID to securely store and manage health records online under Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission. Key to your digital healthcare journey

What is ABHA ID?

ABHA number is a 14 digit number that will uniquely identify you as a participant in India's digital healthcare ecosystem. ABHA number will establish a strong and trustable identity for you that will be accepted by healthcare providers and payers across the country

 
Benefits of ABHA

Unique & Trustable Identity

Establish unique identity across different healthcare providers within the healthcare ecosystem

Unified Benefits

Link all healthcare benefits ranging from public health programmes to insurance schemes to your unique ABHA number

Hassle-free Access

Avoid long lines for registration in healthcare facilities across the country

Easy ABHA Address Sign Up

Seamless sign up for ABHA Address (Personal Health Records) applications such as ABDM for Health data sharing

ABHA Fundamentals

What is ABHA Number?

ABHA number is a 14 digit number that will uniquely identify you as a participant in Indias digital healthcare ecosystem. ABHA number will establish a strong and trustable identity for you that will be accepted by healthcare providers and payers across the country

What is ABHA Address?

ABHA (Ayushman Bharat Health Account) Address is a unique identifier ( self-declared username) that enables you to share and access your health records digitally. Your ABHA address may look like yourname@consent manager. For instance, xyz@abdm is an ABHA address with ABDM Consent Manager that will facilitate health data exchange for you with appropriate consent on the ABDM network

Linking ABHA number with ABHA address

You can use your ABHA number to seamlessly sign up for an ABHA address, and ensure that the health records created for you are shared only with you. To enable health data sharing, it is recommended that you create ABDM ABHA address and link it with your ABHA number.

Voluntary Opt-out

You can participate at your own free will and choose to create your ABHA number voluntarily. Also, at any time, you can also request for permanent deletion or temporary deactivation of your ABHA number

What are the Benefits of ABHA Health ID?

ABHA allows you to digitize health records for your family. It has the following advantages

 

  • Digital Health Records

    You can access your health records anywhere with ABHA ID

  • Consent Based Access

    Access to health data is provided only after the user's consent. User have the right to manage and revoke consent when required.

  • Security

    The platform is highly secure and encrypted. The data is shared only by user's consent.

  • Voluntary Opt In and Opt Out

    You can register at your own will and can delete data at any point of time

  • PHR(Personal Health Records)

    You can access and link your Personal Health Records (PHR) with ABHA to create a health history.

  • Easy Sign Up

    You can create an ABHA ID with your basic details using a mobile or Aadhaar number.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is ABHA?
Under Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM), individuals can create ABHA ID (Ayushman Bharat Health Account) to manage their health records. ABHA ID is a 14 digit unique number to identify the users.
What is the Full form of PHR?
The full form of PHR is Personal Health Record.
What is a National Health ID?
National Health ID is an alternative name of ABHA Id issued by ABDM.
What is a Health ID?
Health ID is the ABHA ID issued under ABDM for digitization of medical records.
How many digits are there in ABHA ID?
ABHA ID is a 14 digit number for one's identification in India's digital health ecosystem.
Does the ABHA ID Card Expire?
No, the ABHA ID Card does not expire. Its validity is life long.
Is ABHA Mandatory?
No, Creating ABHA is not mandatory.
Can children below 18 years also create ABHA ID?
Yes, ABHA can be created for children below 18 years.
Is ABHA free?
Yes, creation of ABHA ID is free.
Is ABHA valid in other countries?
No, ABHA is valid only in India.
Does the ABHA ID card need a renewal?
No, the ABHA ID card need not be renewed.
Is ABHA valid outside government hospitals/programs?
Yes, ABHA is valid outside the government as well.
Can I delete my ABHA number (Health ID)?
Yes, you can delete your ABHA or Health ID permanently or deactivate it temporarily. This will enable you to opt-out of the digital health ecosystem as you may require.
How to link an Aadhaar Card with ABHA ID?
When you create your ABHA ID, your Aadhaar Card is automatically linked with it.
How to link an ABHA card with a mobile number?
When you create an ABHA ID, the mobile number automatically gets linked to the Aadhaar Card.
What is the use of ABHA Health ID?
You can use ABHA ID to digitize your health records and share it with family, friends, hospitals, doctors and clinics when desired.
What documents are required for creating an ABHA Card?
You only need a mobile number or a Driving License to apply for an Aadhaar card. No physical documents are required for the same.
Is ABHA applicable to everyone?
Yes, ABHA is applicable to everyone who is a citizen of India.
